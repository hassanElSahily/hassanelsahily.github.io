Sustainability & Civic Engagement

This section highlights my commitment to social responsibility, humanitarian engagement, and sustainable values. Beyond technical skills, I strongly believe that engineers have a role to play in supporting communities and contributing to collective well-being.

Humanitarian Engagement — Red Cross Lebanon

From 2021 to 2022, I was actively involved as a volunteer with the Lebanese Red Cross, where I contributed to humanitarian and civic actions in a challenging social and economic context.

  • Coordination of food distribution events for vulnerable populations.
  • Participation in logistical organization and on-field operations.
  • Awareness and educational activities with young people on life skills, solidarity, and civic values.

Building on this experience, I will be returning this summer to continue my engagement with the Lebanese Red Cross, reinforcing my long-term commitment to humanitarian action and civic responsibility. Which also validates my 30-40h of civic engagement.

Values and Impact

These experiences strengthened my sense of responsibility, teamwork, and leadership, while reinforcing the importance of empathy and adaptability in complex environments.

They also shape my vision of engineering as a discipline that should serve people, sustainability, and social progress.
