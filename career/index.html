Career Development

I am currently specializing in computer systems, architecture, and networks, with a strong academic interest in low-level systems, infrastructure, and the foundations that enable reliable and scalable computing.

Beyond engineering practice, I aspire to pursue a PhD and build a long-term career in academia. My objective is to contribute to scientific research while engaging in university-level teaching, as I strongly believe in the role of education as a driver of inspiration, critical thinking, and intellectual growth.

I am particularly motivated by research in computer science, where scientific work not only advances technology, but also makes knowledge, tools, and innovation accessible to a wider community. Through research and teaching, I aim to contribute to the greater good by supporting open knowledge, scientific rigor, and the transmission of expertise to future generations.
