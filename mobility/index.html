Personal & Cultural Experience (PCE)

Through the Personal & Cultural Experience component of my studies, supported by sustained interaction with international environments, I strengthened my adaptability, intercultural communication skills, and capacity to integrate efficiently into new professional and cultural contexts.

In particular, I completed the course Intercultural Management delivered by ESCP, which provided me with structured tools to analyze cultural differences, manage cross-cultural teams, and understand how cultural contexts influence decision-making and organizational behavior.

I also followed the course Inclusive Leadership: The Power of Workplace Diversity offered by the University of Colorado, strengthening my understanding of inclusive leadership, unconscious bias, and the value of diversity in fostering innovation, collaboration, and sustainable team performance.

Altogether, these experiences reinforced my adaptability, open-mindedness, and ability to collaborate effectively in multicultural and inclusive environments, essential competencies for an engineer operating in a global context.
